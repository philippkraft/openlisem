Calibration parameters

A value of 1.0 means that all input maps are used as they are. The value is a multiplication factor, larger than 1 increasees the map value, smaller than 1 decreases the map value. This has different effects:

- Ksat slope and channel: increase means more infiltration and less runoff/discharge.

- Theta and Psi: increase means a wetter soil and less infiltration, more runoff.

- Manning N slope and Channel: increase means more friction, slower runoff and generally also less runoff (more time for infiltration).
