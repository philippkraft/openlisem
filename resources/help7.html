Calibration parameters

A value of 1.0 means that all input maps are used as they are. The value is a multiplication factor, larger than 1 increasees the map value, smaller than 1 decreases the map value.The effect is a matter of trial and error, a complex landscaope can behave in different ways, and scale is also important. In general

  • Ksat slope and channel: increase means more infiltration, less runoff.
  • Theta: increase means a wetter soil and less infiltration, more runoff.
  • Psi: increase means a drier soil with more suction, less runoff.
  • Manning N slope and Channel: increase means more friction, slower runoff and channel response.
  • Cohesion and Aggregate stability: increase means a stronger soil and less detachment.
    • NOTE: Channel cohesion can be set to -1, in which case there is no flow detachment (but there is transport and deposition). This can be used for concrete channels for instance.
  • D50: median grainsize for suspended matter. Influences the transport capacity and deposition. A larger value generally means a higher sediment flux, but also faster deposition
  • D90, the same as D50 but for bedload sediment tarsnport

Note: less runoff means less erosion.
